FSN E-Commerce Ventures Ltd, doing business as Nykaa, is an Indian retail company, headquartered in Mumbai. It sells beauty, wellness and fashion products through its website, mobile app and over 100 physical stores.[4] In 2020, it became the first Indian unicorn startup headed by a woman.[5]

Nykaa was listed on the National Stock Exchange and Bombay Stock Exchange in November 2021 following its initial public offering.[6]

History

[edit]

Nykaa was founded in April 2012 by Falguni Nayar, a former investment banker and Managing Director at Kotak Mahindra Capital.[7] The company launched as an online beauty retailer in 2012 and began commercial operations in 2013. Its first offline store opened in the Delhi airport in 2014.[8][9]

In 2015, Nykaa launched its own brands with Nykaa Cosmetics. Its owned brands would later develop into its House of Nykaa business.[10][11]

In 2018, Nykaa expanded into fashion with the launch of Nykaa Fashion.[12][13]

In March 2020, Nykaa became India’s first woman-led unicorn after raising capital from Steadview Capital at a valuation of approximately ₹102 crore (US$12 million) US$1.2 billion.[14][15] In November 2020, global asset management firm Fidelity invested in the company through a secondary sale of shares from multiple equity sellers.[16]

In 2020, the company announced the launch of Nykaa Man the same year, targeting men’s grooming and fashion.[17][18]

In December 2020, Nykaa Fashion launched its first store in Delhi.[19][20]

Nykaa opened its initial public offering (IPO) in late October 2021 and was listed on the NSE and BSE on 10 November 2021.[21] The IPO raised ₹5,352 crore (US$724.05 million) at a valuation of US$7.4 billion.[22] Following the listing, founder Falguni Nayar became India’s wealthiest self-made female billionaire.[23]

In 2022, Nykaa partnered with Apparel Group to sell beauty products in the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, Kuwait and Bahrain.[24][25] In 2024, Nykaa and the Apparel Group entered a 55:45 joint venture to launch Nysaa in Gulf countries.[26][27][28]

Operations

[edit]

Nykaa operates a hybrid model, with an inventory-led approach for beauty and personal care products and a marketplace model for fashion.[29][30][31]

It has three offline store formats called Nykaa Luxe, Nykaa On Trend and Nykaa beauty kiosks. The Luxe format features international luxury beauty brands such as Huda Beauty, MAC, Dior, and Givenchy, along with Nykaa Beauty, the in-house collection of beauty products.[32] The Nykaa On Trend sells curated products.[33]

Nykaa has retail and distribution subsidiaries in the Gulf region, including Nysaa Cosmetics Trading in Qatar for the export and retail sale of beauty and personal care products, and Nysaa Trading LLC in Saudi Arabia.[34]

House of brands

[edit]

Nykaa has in-house brands within beauty and fashion. Some of them include:

  • Nykaa House of Brands – Nykaa Naturals, Nykaa Cosmetics, Kay Beauty
  • Nykaa Fashion – Nykd by Nykaa, 20 Dresses, RSVP, Mondano, Likha,[35] Pipa Bella[36][37]

In 2015, Nykaa launched its collection of in-house beauty products via Nykaa Cosmetics.[38][39] Nykaa Naturals sells skincare and personal care products.[40] In early 2019, the brand launched its Wanderlust Bath & Body collection, and later in the year introduced a beauty line with designer Masaba Gupta, Masaba by Nykaa.[41][42] The same year, it launched its first celebrity partnership brand, Kay Beauty, with actress Katrina Kaif.[43][44]

Acquisitions

[edit]

In May 2019, Nykaa acquired 20Dresses.com, a private women's styling platform.[45]

In 2021, Nykaa Fashion acquired the India fashion jewellery brand, Pipa Bella,[46] and the Indian skincare brand, Dot & Key.[47]

In 2022, Nykaa acquired an 18.51% stake in Indian skincare brand Earth Rhythm.[48] It then completed the 100% acquisition of lifestyle content platform Little Black Book (LBB).[49]

Content platforms

[edit]

Nykaa hosts beauty and fashion content its YouTube channel, Nykaa TV. It contains informational videos about beauty, cosmetics, and styling.[50]

Since 2018, it has been hosting an online community of beauty and fashion enthusiasts via its Nykaa Network. In 2020, it launched a web miniseries called The Beauty Bar. Nykaa also hosts Beauty Book, a beauty and fashion magazine.[citation needed]

Other activities

[edit]

Since 2015, Nykaa has hosted the ‘Nykaa Femina Beauty Awards’ in partnership with women's lifestyle magazine Femina (India).[51]

In 2019, Nykaa Fashion launched ‘The Power List’ in partnership with Vogue India.[citation needed]

Criticism

[edit]

Security issues

[edit]

On 18 November 2019, an API flaw was detected in Nykaa Fashion's internal systems that allowed a potential attacker to log in to any user account if the attacker had access to the user's email ID.[52][53] Consequently, the company fixed the security vulnerability.[52]

Corporate governance

[edit]

In November 2022, Nykaa issued 5:1 bonus shares to coincide with the expiry date of pre-IPO shareholders' lock-in period. The bonus issue resulted in the company's corporate governance practices receiving heavy criticism, with questions raised over the timing and motive of the issuance along with concerns of market manipulation and tax avoidance.[54][55] Later that month, CFO Arvind Agarwal resigned from the company,[56] even as the Securities and Exchange Board of India began scrutinizing the allotment of bonus shares.[57]

In February 2023, defending the bonus share issuance, CEO Falguni Nayar, a former investment banker, claimed that the company had "assumed shareholders would be able to start trading the bonus shares within 4-5 days of the shareholder voting on the issue."[58]
